A Legacy Crafted Over 21 Years

Founded by Tseday Asrat and Elias Ketema in 2005, Kaldi's Coffee opened its very first storefront near Edna Mall in the vibrant Bole district of Addis Ababa. What began as a single ambitious café has flourished into a beloved national icon.

Our identity honors Ethiopian history. We take our name from Kaldi, the legendary 9th-century Ethiopian herder who famously discovered the invigorating properties of coffee beans when his goats began dancing after eating them.
